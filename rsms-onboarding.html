  1. DATA OWNERSHIP: All student, staff and academic data entered into RSMS belongs exclusively to the School. Rehoteq Technologies will not access, share or sell school data without explicit written consent.
  2. DATA PRIVACY: All student NIN and personal information will be stored securely in encrypted form. The LIN field is reserved for Federal Government integration as per DNEMIS requirements.
  3. SERVICE UPTIME: Rehoteq Technologies will make reasonable effort to maintain system availability of at least 95% uptime during each school term.
  4. PAYMENT TERMS: The School agrees to pay the agreed subscription fee before each term begins. Failure to pay within 14 days of term commencement may result in read-only access until payment is made.

  7. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, the School's data will be exported and provided to them in CSV format within 7 working days.
  8. UPDATES: Software updates are provided free of charge throughout the subscription period.
  9. ACCEPTABLE USE: The School agrees not to use RSMS for any unlawful purpose, and to ensure all data entered is accurate and truthful.
  10. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: RSMS software, code and design remain the exclusive property of Rehoteq Technologies. The School is granted a non-exclusive licence to use the system during their subscription period.
  11. GOVERNMENT COMPLIANCE: The School agrees to provide DNEMIS school codes and student LINs as required by the Federal Ministry of Education, and to keep such records updated within RSMS.
