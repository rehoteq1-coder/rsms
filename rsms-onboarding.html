2. SERVICES
The Provider agrees to supply the Rehoteq School Management System (RSMS) which includes student management, results, attendance, fee tracking, CBT exams, and parent portals based on the selected plan.
3. PAYMENT TERMS
The School agrees to pay the one-time onboarding fee before activation. Term subscription fees are due within 14 days of each new school term. Access becomes read-only after the grace period.
4. DATA OWNERSHIP
All school data remains the property of the School. Rehoteq Technologies will not share or sell school data to third parties.
5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to keep login credentials and operational data confidential.
6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. The School's data will be available for export for 30 days after termination.
